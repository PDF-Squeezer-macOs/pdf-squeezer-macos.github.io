PDF Squeezer

A simple-to-use PDF compression tool for macOS. Drag and drop your files to reduce size by compressing images and removing irrelevant data—saving bandwidth, storage, and transmission time.

Drag and Drop Simplicity

Drop any PDF into the app window. No complex setup required.

Predefined Filters or Custom

Batch and Folder Processing

Compress multiple files at once, or entire folders including subfolders.

Seamless Integration

One-Click Compression

How It Works

Step 1: Drag Files

Pull PDFs into the window or use share menu from Finder/Safari/Preview.

Step 2: Choose Filter

Pick a preset or customize compression settings.

Step 3: Compress

Hit compress for single files or batches. Handles folders and passwords effortlessly.

Step 4: Save

Review results and save optimized PDFs automatically with one click.
